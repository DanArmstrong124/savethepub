Who is Time At The Bar?

Time at the Bar is a Pub and Cafe application developed and available on all devices, however predominantly mobiles and tablets. The app is available from both Android and IOS stores.
Time at the Bar (TAB) is available for you the user as an App to assist you with finding:


- The nearest bar or cafe using your location services
- Venues that meet your search criteria by using thousands of key features provided by our members, pubs, bars, clubs, hotels, cafes etc…
- Whether you’re looking for a night out with your partner, friends or family, our TAB app can assist you.
